historical identity stationary

For this project, I had to choose from a list of historical figures(real or myth) and make a logo for them. This logo had to relate to who they were as a person. I choose the greek god Zeus because I love greek mythology. I tried to stear away from the typical zeus logo. As I was researching him, I learned one of his symbols was a bull. Since he was know for his anger and strength it seemed fitting.
